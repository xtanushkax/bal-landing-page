About Me

Hi, I'm a participant in the Breakthrough Author Live hackathon. I'm passionate about building beautiful, high-converting web experiences that help coaches share their message with the world.

This landing page was built entirely with Claude Code during the BAL hackathon — no Googling, no frameworks, just plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. Every section, animation, and responsive breakpoint was crafted through conversation with AI.

I believe that great design serves a purpose: to connect the right message with the right people. This project is a testament to what's possible when you combine human creativity with AI-powered development.
